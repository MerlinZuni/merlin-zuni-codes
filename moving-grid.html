Rotating Clock

This clock was built as an experiment in rotation with cos() & sin().

Rotating Parts

The clock is broken into different time increments. Hours, minutes & seconds.

Hours

The hours are based on 12 hours for the innermost rotation of the grey hour hand.

Minutes

This part of the hand is based on 60 minutes, the minute arm is red.

Seconds

The seconds hand is the last blue part of the arm.
